Privacy Policy for Campa Parking

Last updated: June 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Campa Parking is an internal application for managing and locating vehicles within dealerships. We are committed to protecting our users' privacy and handling their data responsibly.

2. Use of the Camera

The application requires access to the device camera for the following purposes:

  • Taking photos of vehicles for identification and record-keeping.
  • Taking profile pictures of users.
  • Automatically scanning license plates and VINs to speed up data entry.

Camera access is used only with user authorization and exclusively for the purposes described above.

3. Management of Images and Data

Captured images are securely stored and used solely for internal dealership management purposes.

4. Security and Privacy

Personal data and images are handled with strict security measures and are accessible only to authorized company personnel.

5. Contact

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact the application administrator.
